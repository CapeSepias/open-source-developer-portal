# Dwolla.js

## Using Dwolla.js for Instant Account Verification (IAV)
For white label partners, `dwolla.js` has the added function of facilitating Instant Account Verification (IAV) on their Customer's bank or credit union account. By calling a separate function, `dwolla.iav.start()` the white label partner application can render the IAV flow within a specified container.
